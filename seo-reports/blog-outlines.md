**Working title (EN):** Which Lash Extension Style is Right for You? Eye Shape → Lash Style Match Guide

**Target keywords:**
- Primary: `ต่อขนตาแบบไหนดี` (350/mo, KD 33)
- Secondary: `ทรงต่อขนตา` (350/mo, KD 0), `ต่อขนตา ตาชั้นเดียว` (longtail), `ทรงขนตา` (1,100/mo, KD 54)

**Format:** Comparison/visual guide. Heavy on images. Mirrors the structure of `takecarebeauty.com` (current SERP #2 with DR 25 and 1,864/mo traffic).

**Length:** ~1,200-1,800 words / ~10-min read

**Outline:**

1. **Hook (intro, 2-3 sentences)**
   - Briefly: "First time getting lashes? Choosing the right style matters more than the price."
   - Set up the eye-shape framework

2. **Quick reference table** (above the fold for scanners)
   - Eye shape → Recommended style (Classic/Volume/Wispy/etc.)
   - Direct CTA buttons to relevant service

3. **Section per eye shape** (5-6 sections, each ~150 words)
   - **ตาชั้นเดียว / Single eyelid (monolid)** → Wispy Volume or Light Volume (lifts the eye)
   - **ตาสองชั้น / Double eyelid** → Classic Volume or Hybrid (any style works)
   - **ตาตก / Downturned eyes** → Cat Eye styling (Mega Volume with heavier outer)
   - **ตากลม / Round eyes** → Doll Eye styling (Russian Volume with heavier center)
   - **ตาหลบใน / Hooded eyes** → Wispy Volume (visible despite hood)
   - **ตาห่าง / Wide-set eyes** → Heavier inner corners (Hybrid or Classic Volume)
   
   Each section includes:
   - 1 photo of an example client (use existing service photos)
   - "Why this works" (1-2 sentences)
   - Direct link to the recommended service page

4. **Style-by-style breakdown** (overview of her 10 services)
   - Table: Style name (TH + EN) → Drama level → Best for → Price
   - Brief one-liner per service

5. **Other factors to consider**
   - Daily lifestyle (active vs office)
   - Maintenance budget (refill frequency)
   - First-time vs experienced

6. **What to expect at your first appointment**
   - Consultation process (Ying actually maps lashes to your eye)
   - Duration (~2 hours for full set)
   - Aftercare basics
   - Mention 5-day guarantee

7. **CTA: Still not sure?**
   - "DM us your eye photo on Instagram and we'll recommend a style"
   - Service menu link

## Blog B — Segment 2 (Switchers): "How long do lash extensions last? + Our 5-day guarantee"

**Working title (TH):** ต่อขนตาอยู่ได้นานแค่ไหน? คำตอบจากช่างมืออาชีพ + รับประกันรีทัช 5 วัน
**Working title (EN):** How Long Do Lash Extensions Last? An Honest Answer + Our 5-Day Retouch Guarantee

**Target keywords:**
- Primary: `ต่อขนตาอยู่ได้นานไหม` (250/mo, KD 0), `ต่อขนตาอยู่ได้นานแค่ไหน` (50/mo)
- Secondary: `ต่อขนตา ข้อเสีย` (350/mo, KD 43), `วิธีดูแลขนตาหลังต่อ` (40/mo, KD 30)
- Cluster volume: ~775/mo combined

**Search intent:** Informational. Reader has had lashes before, fell out fast, looking for a better shop.

**Format:** Direct-answer + trust-builder. Optimized for **Google AI Overview citation** (currently #1 SERP feature for this query — owning the AI Overview is the prize).

**Length:** ~900-1,300 words / ~7-min read

**Outline:**

1. **The direct answer (first paragraph — critical for AI Overview)**
   - "With proper care, professionally applied lash extensions last 3–4 weeks before needing a refill. A natural lash sheds every 60-90 days, taking the extension with it."
   - Format this as a clean, citable paragraph — short, factual, linkable

2. **What actually affects how long they last** (5 factors, each 1 paragraph)
   - Glue quality (premium vs cheap)
   - Application technique (handmade vs premade fans, isolation skill)
   - Aftercare (the customer side — see section 4)
   - Skin type (oily skin sheds extensions faster)
   - Lash health going in (damaged lashes hold less)

3. **Why some shops' lashes only last 1 week (the honest part)**
   - Premade fans = heavier, fall out faster
   - Cheap glue = breaks down with humidity (relevant for Chiang Mai)
   - Rushed isolation = clusters that pull out together
   - Position My Lash House: "We use only handmade fans, premium glue, and never rush isolation"

4. **Aftercare guide — what YOU control** (the most-asked-about practical advice)
   - First 24 hours: no water, no steam
   - First week: avoid oil-based skincare/makeup near eyes
   - Daily: clean with lash shampoo, brush gently
   - Sleeping: silk pillowcase, sleep on back
   - What to avoid: rubbing, mascara, eyelash curlers

5. **Our 5-Day Retouch Guarantee** (the differentiator)
   - **What's covered:** abnormal shedding due to glue failure within 5 days
   - **What's not covered:** rubbing, premature water exposure, oil-based products
   - **How to claim:** message us on Instagram with photos within 5 days
   - **What you get:** free retouch appointment
   - **Why we offer this:** "Because if our lashes don't last, we want to fix it — not your problem"

6. **When to book your refill**
   - Sweet spot: 2-3 weeks
   - Sign you waited too long: more than 50% gaps
   - Refill pricing reference

7. **CTA:** Try us with peace of mind — book on Instagram

**FAQ section at bottom (FAQPage schema):**
- Q: How long should lash extensions last?
- Q: Why did my last lashes fall out so fast?
- Q: What's covered by your retouch guarantee?
- Q: How often should I get a refill?

**Internal linking targets:**
- 5-day guarantee details (this becomes the canonical source — link from Hero badge, FAQ, Service pages)
- Aftercare-focused service pages (Refill, Removal)
- Pricing
- Blog A (style guide) when published

**Image needs:**
- Header image: clean lash close-up
- Aftercare visual checklist (could be a simple infographic)
- Before/after at 2 weeks vs 4 weeks (illustrates natural shedding)
