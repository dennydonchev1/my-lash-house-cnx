## 1. Executive summary

1. **The Chiang Mai lash market on the open web is essentially uncontested.** The top "Chiang Mai-targeted" website competitor (Angels Eyelash Extensions, DR 17) ranks for only 9 keywords with ~14 monthly visits. Local pack and Facebook/Instagram dominate page-1 SERPs — beatable with a real website + GBP.
2. **Two bilingual strategies, two segments:**
   - **Segment 1 (first-timers):** "ต่อขนตาแบบไหนดี" + "ทรงต่อขนตา" cluster — content/comparison play, ~700-1,500 monthly volume combined
   - **Segment 2 (switchers):** "ต่อขนตา อยู่ได้นานไหม" + "ต่อขนตา ข้อเสีย" cluster — trust/longevity play, ~700-900 monthly volume, lots of zero-difficulty terms
3. **Local pack is the prize, not website rankings.** "near me" Thai queries (~14,800/mo combined) are decided by GBP signals, not on-page SEO. Reinforces existing strategy: GBP optimization > content for foot-traffic.
4. **AI Overview is already showing for longevity questions.** The "ต่อขนตาอยู่ได้นานไหม" SERP shows Google's AI Overview at position #1 — content optimized for AI Overview citation has outsized leverage for this query cluster.
5. **No Brand Radar / LLM data available** — Ahrefs Lite plan doesn't include the Brand Radar addon. Recommend deferring AI search visibility tracking until brand has 3+ months of organic presence (currently mylashhouse.com is ~24 hours old).
